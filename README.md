# Beyond Hallucination: AI Dialects and Private Standards of Judgment

This repository hosts a conceptual report on **AI dialects** and **private standards of judgment** in long-term human-AI interaction.

The report proposes that AI risks should not be understood only in terms of hallucination or low-quality output. Long-term interaction with AI systems may also create private meanings, project-specific terminology, and tacit standards of adequacy that users may mistake for public language or general standards.

## Core Claim

> Hallucination produces false claims.
> AI dialect produces private meanings.
> Judgment dialect produces private standards of adequacy.

## Note

“AI dialect,” “linguistic dialect,” and “judgment dialect” are proposed working terms in this essay, not established academic categories.
